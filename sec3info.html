The lessons are suitable for G2 Elective / Pure History students because the skills and content covered are largely similar.

Content: G2 Pure History students cover the same topics tested in our G3 Elective History mock exam. G2 Elective History students cover the same topics as well, except for Militarist Japan.

Source-based skills: G2 Elective & Pure History students are tested on the same source-based skills as G3 students.

Essays: G2 essay questions are similar to G3 essay questions, but G2 students are marked differently. G2 students are only required to explain two perspectives, unlike G3 students who will need to show evaluation in their essays.
